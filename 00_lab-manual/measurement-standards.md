# Measurement Standards
## Default units
- Weigh ingredients in **grams** when possible.
- Temps in **°F** (include °C if you prefer).
- Times in minutes.

## Common conversions
- 1 tbsp = 15 mL
- 1 tsp = 5 mL
- Pinch/splash: avoid in protocols unless you include a weight range.

## Naming
- Prefer explicit: “kosher salt (Diamond Crystal)” or “fine sea salt”.
- Write “lemon (zest + juice)” instead of “lemon” if both are used.
