# Dish: Miso-ish Chicken Thigh Bowl

**Tags:** #weeknight #japanese-ish #high-protein  
**Prep time:** 10 min  
**Cook time:** 35–45 min  
**Total time:** 45–55 min  
**Active time:** ~15 min  
**Servings:** 2–4  
**Tools:** oven, sheet pan, rice cooker, saucepan  
**Health goals:** #high-protein  
**Macros per serving (estimate):** optional (add later)

## What it is
Crispy chicken thighs glazed with soy-ginger, served over rice with tender broccoli.

## Uses protocols
- [Sheet-Pan Chicken Thighs](../01_protocols/proteins/sheet-pan-chicken-thighs.md)
- [Quick Soy-Ginger Glaze](../01_protocols/sauces/quick-soy-ginger-glaze.md)
- [Rice Cooker Jasmine Rice](../01_protocols/starches/rice-cooker-jasmine-rice.md)
- [Tender Broccoli (Steam + Finish)](../01_protocols/veg-sides/tender-broccoli_steam-finish.md)

## Steps (high level)
1. Start rice.
2. Roast chicken thighs.
3. Make glaze while chicken cooks.
4. Steam broccoli; finish with oil/lemon.
5. Glaze chicken in last 5 minutes or brush on after resting.

## Plating
Rice → broccoli → sliced chicken → drizzle extra glaze.

## Variations
- Add sesame + scallions.
- Swap broccoli for roasted carrots.
