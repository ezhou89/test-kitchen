# Protocol: Rice Cooker Jasmine Rice

**Category:** starches  
**Tags:** #weeknight #staple #rice-cooker  
**Version:** v0.1
**Created:** 2025-12-26
**Servings:** 2–4  
**Prep time:** 5 min  
**Cook time:** per machine (~30–45 min)  
**Tools:** rice cooker, fine mesh strainer (optional)

## Goal / Why this exists
Repeatable rice for bowls, curries, and quick dinners.

## Ingredients
- 300 g jasmine rice
- Water (use rice cooker markings, or start with 1:1 by weight and adjust to taste)
- Salt (optional): 2–3 g

## Method
1. Rinse rice until water runs mostly clear (optional but helps texture).
2. Add rice + water to cooker. Add salt if using.
3. Cook on standard white rice setting.
4. Rest 10 min on warm; fluff.

## Success criteria
- Fluffy grains, not gummy, not crunchy.
