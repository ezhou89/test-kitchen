# Protocol: Lemon-Herb Vinaigrette

**Category:** sauces  
**Tags:** #weeknight #low-mess #bright #salad #veg  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Yield:** ~120 g (about 8 servings at 1 tbsp)  
**Prep time:** 5 min  
**Cook time:** 0 min  
**Tools:** bowl, whisk (or jar), digital scale (optional)

## Goal / Why this exists
A fast, flexible vinaigrette that upgrades vegetables, salads, chicken, and grain bowls.

## Ingredients
- 60 g extra-virgin olive oil
- 20 g lemon juice
- 5 g Dijon mustard
- 5 g honey (or maple)
- 3 g kosher salt (adjust)
- 1 g black pepper
- 5–10 g minced herbs (parsley, dill, chives)

## Method
1. Whisk lemon juice, mustard, honey, salt, pepper.
2. Whisk in olive oil to emulsify.
3. Stir in herbs. Taste and adjust salt/acid.

## Success criteria
- Bright, balanced (not harsh), coats a spoon lightly.

## Variations
- Add garlic (microplaned) for more punch.
- Swap lemon → rice vinegar for a softer profile.

## Storage & reheat
- Refrigerate up to 5 days. Re-whisk or shake before using.
