# Protocol: Quick Soy-Ginger Glaze

**Category:** sauces  
**Tags:** #weeknight #japanese-ish #glaze #low-mess  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Yield:** ~150 g  
**Prep time:** 5 min  
**Cook time:** 6–8 min  
**Tools:** small saucepan, whisk

## Goal / Why this exists
A fast stovetop glaze for chicken, salmon, tofu, roasted veg, or rice bowls.

## Ingredients
- 80 g soy sauce
- 30 g mirin (or 20 g sugar + 10 g water)
- 20 g rice vinegar
- 10 g grated ginger
- 1–2 cloves garlic, microplaned (optional)
- 5 g cornstarch + 10 g water (slurry)

## Method
1. Bring soy, mirin, vinegar, ginger (and garlic) to a simmer.
2. Simmer 3–5 min.
3. Whisk in slurry; simmer 1–2 min until glossy.

## Success criteria
- Glossy, lightly coats a spoon, not gummy.

## Variations
- Add chili flakes for heat.
- Add toasted sesame oil off-heat.

## Storage
- Fridge 5 days. Rewarm gently.
