# Protocol: Roasted Carrots (Cumin + Honey)

**Category:** veg-sides  
**Tags:** #weeknight #sheet-pan #sweet-savory #veg  
**Version:** v0.1
**Created:** 2025-12-26
**Servings:** 2–4  
**Prep time:** 10 min  
**Cook time:** 20–30 min  
**Tools:** sheet pan

## Goal / Why this exists
A dependable roasted side that plays well with chicken, fish, and grain bowls.

## Ingredients
- 600 g carrots (coins or batons)
- 15 g olive oil
- 5 g honey
- 2 g ground cumin
- 4 g kosher salt
- Black pepper

## Method
1. Heat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss carrots with oil, honey, cumin, salt, pepper.
3. Roast 20–30 min, toss once, until browned and tender.

## Success criteria
- Browning on edges, tender center, not shriveled.
