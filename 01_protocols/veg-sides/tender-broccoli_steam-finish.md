# Protocol: Tender Broccoli (Steam + Finish)

**Category:** veg-sides  
**Tags:** #weeknight #low-mess #tender #veg  
**Version:** v0.1
**Created:** 2025-12-26
**Servings:** 2–4  
**Prep time:** 5 min  
**Cook time:** 6–8 min  
**Tools:** pot with lid (or steamer), bowl

## Goal / Why this exists
Tender broccoli without “over-roasting.” Great for sauces, glazes, and vinaigrettes.

## Ingredients
- 500 g broccoli florets
- 6 g kosher salt (divided)
- 10–15 g olive oil or butter
- 10 g lemon juice (optional)

## Method
1. Add 1–2 cm water to pot; bring to simmer.
2. Add broccoli + a pinch of salt. Cover and steam 4–6 min until tender-crisp.
3. Drain well. Toss with oil/butter, remaining salt, and lemon if using.

## Success criteria
- Bright green, tender but not falling apart, no watery puddle.

## Variations
- Toss with [Lemon-Herb Vinaigrette](../sauces/lemon-herb-vinaigrette.md).
