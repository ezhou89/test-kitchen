# Protocol: Cookies and Cream Ice Cream (Ninja Creami)

**Category:** desserts
**Tags:** #high-protein #batch-prep #low-mess
**Version:** v0.1
**Servings / Yield:** 1 pint (4 servings, ~120g each)
**Prep time:** 5 min
**Freeze time:** 24 hours
**Tools:** Ninja Creami, blender or shaker bottle

## Goal / Why this exists
Classic cookies and cream flavor with high protein. The Oreo-style cookies add crunch and nostalgia while keeping calories reasonable. One of the most satisfying comfort food Creami recipes.

## Nutrition (per serving, ~130g)
- **Calories:** ~140
- **Protein:** ~18g
- **Carbs:** ~12g
- **Fat:** ~3g

## Ingredients

### Base
- 240g whole milk
- 50g vanilla protein powder (~1.75 scoops, casein or blend preferred)
- 15g granulated sweetener (monk fruit or allulose)
- Pinch kosher salt

### Mix-ins (added after processing)
- 40g chocolate sandwich cookies (Oreos or similar), roughly crushed

## Method
1. **Combine** milk, protein powder, sweetener, and salt in a blender or shaker. Blend until smooth.
2. **Pour** into Ninja Creami pint container to max fill line.
3. **Freeze** for 24 hours minimum. Must be frozen solid.
4. **Process** on ICE CREAM setting.
5. **Create a hole** in the center of the processed ice cream. Add crushed cookies.
6. **Process** on MIX-IN setting to distribute cookie pieces throughout.
7. **Serve immediately** for soft-serve with crunchy cookie bits.

## Success criteria
- Sweet vanilla base, not protein-y
- Visible cookie chunks throughout
- Cookie pieces maintain some crunch (soften slightly is OK)

## Variations
- **Double Stuf:** Use 50g cookies for more cookie flavor
- **Mint cookies and cream:** Use mint Oreos or add 1/4 tsp peppermint extract to base
- **Birthday cake:** Add 20g rainbow sprinkles with the cookies

## Storage & reheat
- Keeps in freezer up to 2 weeks
- Cookie pieces will soften over time in freezer; best eaten within a few days
- RE-SPIN if frozen solid
