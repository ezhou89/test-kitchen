# Protocol: Sheet-Pan Chicken Thighs (Crispy Skin)

**Category:** proteins  
**Tags:** #weeknight #low-mess #high-protein #sheet-pan  
**Version:** v0.1
**Created:** 2025-12-26
**Servings:** 2–4  
**Prep time:** 10 min  
**Cook time:** 30–40 min  
**Tools:** sheet pan, rack (optional), instant-read thermometer

## Goal / Why this exists
Reliable crispy skin and juicy thighs with minimal cleanup.

## Ingredients
- 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- 8 g kosher salt (about 2 g per thigh; adjust)
- 2 g black pepper
- Optional: 2 g garlic powder, 2 g paprika

## Method
1. Heat oven to **425°F**.
2. Pat thighs dry. Season all over.
3. Place skin-side up on sheet pan (rack if you have it).
4. Roast 30–40 min until **175–195°F** in the thickest part (thighs like higher temps).
5. Rest 5 min.

## Success criteria
- Skin is crisp, meat is juicy, no pink near bone.

## Variations
- Brush with [Quick Soy-Ginger Glaze](../sauces/quick-soy-ginger-glaze.md) in the last 5 minutes.
