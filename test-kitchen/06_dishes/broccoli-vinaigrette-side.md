# Dish: Broccoli + Vinaigrette Side Plate

**Tags:** #weeknight #veg #bright #low-mess  
**Prep time:** 5 min  
**Cook time:** 6–8 min  
**Total time:** 12–15 min  
**Active time:** 10 min  
**Servings:** 2–4  
**Tools:** pot with lid, bowl

## What it is
Tender steamed broccoli finished with lemon-herb vinaigrette.

## Uses protocols
- [Tender Broccoli (Steam + Finish)](../01_protocols/veg-sides/tender-broccoli_steam-finish.md)
- [Lemon-Herb Vinaigrette](../01_protocols/sauces/lemon-herb-vinaigrette.md)

## Steps (high level)
1. Steam broccoli until tender.
2. Toss with vinaigrette (start with 1–2 tbsp; add more to taste).
