# Menu: Weeknight Japanese-ish Dinner (3-course)

**Theme:** Japanese-ish comfort bowl + bright veg  
**Diners:** 2â€“4  
**Time budget:** ~60 minutes (mostly passive)  
**Constraints:** low mess, repeatable  
**Tools required:** rice cooker, sheet pan, saucepan, pot with lid

## Courses
1. Starter: optional miso soup (add later)
2. Main: [Miso-ish Chicken Thigh Bowl](../06_dishes/miso-ish-chicken-thigh-bowl.md)
3. Side: [Broccoli + Vinaigrette Side Plate](../06_dishes/broccoli-vinaigrette-side.md)

## Master timeline (backwards plan)
- T-60: start rice cooker
- T-50: preheat oven; season chicken
- T-45: chicken in oven
- T-20: start glaze
- T-15: steam broccoli; finish + hold warm
- T-5: glaze chicken; rest briefly
- T: bowl assembly + serve

## Shopping list (delta)
- Chicken thighs
- Broccoli
- Jasmine rice
- Ginger
- Lemon
- Herbs (parsley/dill/chives)

## Serve notes
- Rice holds on warm for a long time.
- Glaze can be made early; rewarm gently.
